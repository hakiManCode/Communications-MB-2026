Improve Your Footwork

Great table tennis starts with strong footwork. Learn drills that help you move quickly around the table, maintain balance, and set up your next shot with precision. Always stay light on your feet as shots can come from any direction and really fast! The foot is similar to actual tennis, but faster on the toes and agility-wise. With improved footwork, you can reach those tricky shots and keep your rallies going longer. Try the ladder drill or shadow play to boost your agility and speed on the court!

Choosing the Right Paddle

From control to speed, the right paddle can change your game. Explore paddle materials, handle grips, and rubber types to find the best option for your playing style. You can get pimple paddles for more control and spin, or smooth rubber for faster shots. Consider your skill level and what you want to improve when picking your paddle.
